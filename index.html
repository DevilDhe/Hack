IF THERE IS A WHEEL,
THERE IS A WAY


Aim

To develop a user-friendly platform and app for persons with disabilities to access information about the accessibility of buildings, offices, public places, and transportation facilities.

Vision

Creating a world where individuals with disabilities can easily access and navigate public spaces, fostering inclusivity and equal opportunities for all.

Mission

Our mission is to build a digital platform that enables individuals with disabilities to share and access information about accessibility features, promoting collaboration and advocacy for a more inclusive society.
